DESCRIPTION

A modern, compact yet beautiful Residential Apartment with Balconies, Elegant Elevation and Functional Spaces

We believe that any form, space or style of design can be enhanced by complementing it with proper color, texture, highlights and overall accent features. Projection Box and the yellow highlights tend to elegantly tie the entire Facade together. Compact spaces need not be very monotonous and boring, Our approach to the tight constraints of site were solved with simple yet functional architectural principles of Balance, Rhythm, Emphasis and the idea of "Form Follows Function", a space need not be overdone for it to glow and simplest of additions can be the real game changer.
